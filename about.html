About us

Moelo Alusteel And Glass is a leading provider of security solutions, offering a comprehensive range of products and services to safeguard residential and commercial properties. Our expertise lies in the production and distribution of high-quality Security Doors & Burglar Bars, Aluminium Doors & Windows, Garage Doors & Staircases, Aluminium Gates & Steel Gates, as well as Palisade Fencing & Wall Spikes.

With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we strive to meet the diverse security needs of our clients. Our team of skilled professionals ensures that all our products are crafted to the highest standards of durability, functionality, and aesthetic appeal. In addition to our product offerings, we also provide repair services to ensure the ongoing reliability and performance of our installations. Whether it's reinforcing security measures or enhancing the visual appeal of your property, Moelo Alusteel And Glass is your trusted partner in safeguarding what matters most.
